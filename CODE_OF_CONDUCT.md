# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want ConfigFoundry to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for
everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or how they found
their way here. This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any other project space.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive, especially in disagreement.
- Assume good faith — most misunderstandings are just that.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully; focus on the work, not the person.
- Be patient with newcomers asking questions that feel basic to you.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks, public or private.
- Discriminatory language or jokes.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unwelcome or unsafe.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the maintainer (see
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for private-contact guidance — the same
channel works for conduct reports). Reports are handled discreetly.
Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that
don't align with this code of conduct, and may warn, temporarily
restrict, or permanently remove contributors for repeated or severe
violations.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests,
discussions) and in any other context where someone is representing
the project.
